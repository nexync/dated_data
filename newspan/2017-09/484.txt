Anne Ross Kiely and William Joseph Connors were married Sep. 23 at St. Luke’s Parish in Darien, Conn. The Rev. David Anderson, an Episcopal priest, performed the ceremony.
Mrs. Connors, 33, is an assistant director in admissions at M.I.T.’s Sloan School of Management in Cambridge, Ma. She graduated from Dickinson College in Carlisle, Pa., and received a master’s degree in education administration from Boston College.
She is the daughter of Robert G. Kiely of Madison, Conn., and the late Andree R. Kiely. The bride’s father retired as the president and chief officer of the Johns Hopkins Anadolu Medical Center in Istanbul. Her mother was a nursing executive at Bridgeport Hospital in Bridgeport, Conn.
Mr. Connors, 36, is a senior director at Fidelity Investments in Boston. He graduated from Babson College in Wellesley, Mass.
He is the son of William Connors of Manchester, Conn., and the late Elaine C. Connors. The groom’s father was the owner of the South Green Tavern in East Hartford, Conn.
The couple, who are avid court tennis players, met in 2013 at the Tennis and Racquet Club in Boston.
