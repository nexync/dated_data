The market for Toronto homes has picked up in recent weeks, with some houses being scooped up after just a few days on the market, brokers say. Buyers who waited to see how effective the Ontario government’s recent measures would be at bursting the real estate bubble, are now entering the market to cautiously make purchases. As the market rebounds, brokers also say they are seeing a slight uptick in bidding wars, but nowhere near as much competition as there was in the first half of the year.
