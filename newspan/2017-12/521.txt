Felicia Carol Goodman and Tal Itzkovich were married Dec. 2 at Deerfield, a golf course in Newark, Del. Rabbi Sanford Dresin officiated.
The bride, 30, is the director of academic and student affairs for the executive M.B.A. programs at Columbia Business School. She graduated, summa cum laude, from Emory University in Atlanta, from which she also received a master of arts degree in history. She also received a master of education degree in sociology and education from the Teachers College, Columbia University.
She is a daughter of Debra A. Goodman and Charles Goodman of Wilmington, Del. The bride’s father is the vice president of revenue integrity at North American Partners in Anesthesia, an anesthesia management company based in Melville, N.Y. 
The groom, 32, works in New York as the head of financial planning and analysis at Tastemade, a media company focused on food, home, and travel based in Santa Monica, Calif. He graduated from Brown and received an M.B.A. from M.I.T.
He is a son of Ahuva Itzkovich and Harold Itzkovich of Brooklyn. The groom’s mother is an assistant principal at the Academy of Finance and Enterprise High School in Long Island City, Queens. His father is the Mid-Atlantic/North East account manager at trans-Pacific Wholesale in Hauppauge, N.Y.
The couple met in April 2014 through mutual friends in New York.
