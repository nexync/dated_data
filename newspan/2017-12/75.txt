Susan Lee Romeo and Peter Friedrich Moesta are to be married Dec. 31 at the Nassau Inn in Princeton, N.J. Carmen Alvarez, an appellate division judge with chambers in Cape May, N.J., is to officiate.
The bride, 59, is a lawyer in Newark for the Office of the Public Defender, Appellate Section. She received a bachelor of arts degree in English literature from Fordham, from which she also received a law degree. She also received a bachelor of science degree in hospitality management from Florida International University.
She is a daughter of Margaret J. Romeo of Melville, N.Y., and the late John P. Romeo.
The groom, 64, is the chief executive officer of Adello Biologics, a biotechnology company, which is based in Piscataway, N.J. He received a master’s in chemistry and a Ph.D. in biochemistry from the Albert Ludwigs University in Freiburg, Germany.
He is a son of Liselotte Moesta of Munich, Germany and the late Hasso Moesta.
The groom’s previous marriage ended in divorce.
The couple met in 2013 through a mutual friend in Princeton.
