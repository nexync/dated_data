Kimberly Carolyn Harris and Daniel Keith Bush were married June 15 in New York. Msgr. Robert T. Ritchie performed the ceremony at St. Patrick’s Cathedral, where he is the rector.
Mrs. Bush, 31, is an enterprise sales manager in New York for Box, an online platform that allows content sharing on the cloud. She graduated from Dartmouth.
She is the daughter of Patricia M. Harris and William E. Harris of Fort Myers, Fla. The bride’s father is the senior vice president for administration at Freeport-McMoRan, the metals exploration and mining company; he works from Fort Myers, and in Jakarta, Indonesia. Her mother was stay-at-home parent.
Mr. Bush, also 31, is known as Keith. He is a business development executive in New York for Apple, the technology company. He graduated from Wake Forest.
He is the son of Christine Irwin Bush and Kim C. Bush of McLean, Va. The groom’s mother was a stay-at-home parent. His father is a consultant, in McLean, on vaccines and other medicine programs in the global health division of the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
The couple met in 2015 when Mr. Bush also worked at Box, and both were in the company’s Austin, Tex., office.
