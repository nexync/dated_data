As with the requirements of mastering any new skill, the Hollins Communications Research Institute’s exercises for teaching clients the physical fundamentals of fluent speech do entail long hours of practice in the clinic, and equal diligence after completion of the initial course. And those seeking a magic bullet can experience frustration. But for thousands of H.C.R.I. alumni, the confidence gained from speaking fluently and the accomplishments it can foster have been a godsend. I know because I’m one of them.
