The Midterm Elections: What Are Your Issues?
Beyond the heat and the noise, substantive issues are at stake as the midterm elections near. What are the top one or two issues that will affect your vote, and why?
Please send your responses — no more than 200 words — by Thursday at 10 a.m., Eastern time; we plan to publish a sampling over the weekend. Include your name, city, state and contact information. Please put “Issues” in the subject line.
