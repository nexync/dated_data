On this week’s podcast, the actress Melanie Lynskey reads “It Took a Villain to Save Our Marriage,” an essay about a couple seeking common ground and finding it in a shared target: their street-parking, two-car-garage-owning neighbor.
The author, Teresa DiFalco, is a writer living in Oregon. You can follow her on Twitter.
Ms. Lynskey’s most recent film is “Little Boxes,” and she starred in the HBO series “Togetherness.” You can also find her on the new Netflix series “Girlboss.” Follow her on Twitter.
