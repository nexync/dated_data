A theater review on Tuesday about the Broadway musical “Amélie” misspelled the given name of the actress who plays the title character. She is Phillipa Soo, not Philippa.
The Pour column last Wednesday, about lesser known wine grapes that are becoming popular, referred incorrectly to Württemberg, Germany, where trollinger is grown. It is a region in southwest Germany, not a city.
An obituary in some editions on Sunday about The New Yorker cartoonist Jack Ziegler, using information from the magazine, misstated the number of New Yorker cartoonists who have died in the last year. It is seven, not six.
