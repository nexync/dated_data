Dr. Ella Anne Damiano, the daughter of Amy Newmark of Greenwich, Conn., and Philip J. Damiano of Amherst, N.H., was married April 22 to Joshua James Elliott, the son of Susan Goldenberg Elliott and Frederick M. Elliott of Milford, Mass. Michael J. Damiano, who received a solemnization certificate from the State of New Hampshire for the occasion, and is a brother of the bride, officiated at Sunny Slope Farm in Alton, N.H.
Dr. Damiano, 28, is an obstetrics-gynecology resident at Dartmouth-Hitchcock Hospital in Lebanon, N.H. She graduated summa cum laude from Georgetown and received a medical degree from Brown.
The bride is the stepdaughter of Melissa S. Damiano and William J. Rouhana Jr.
The bride’s father is an operating partner at J. W. Childs Associates, an investment firm in Waltham, Mass. Her mother is the publisher, editor in chief and an author of the “Chicken Soup for the Soul” book series and consumer goods operation in Greenwich. The bride’s stepfather is the company’s chairman and chief executive.
The bride’s maternal great-grandfather, the late Saul Newmark, was a founder of Newmark & Company Real Estate in New York.
Mr. Elliott, 26, is pursuing a Ph.D. at Dartmouth, where he is focusing on robotics and snow science in the polar regions. Until 2015, he worked as a civil servant on submarine-related technologies for the United States Navy at the Naval Undersea Warfare Center in Newport, R.I. He graduated summa cum laude from Tufts.
The groom’s mother is a writing tutor at Dean College in Franklin, Mass. His father is based in Milford as an independent property manager of several multifamily buildings in central Massachusetts.
The couple met online in fall 2014, and a week later had dinner at a restaurant in Providence, R.I.
