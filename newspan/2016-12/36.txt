Kate Elizabeth Ottaviano, the daughter of Catherine A. Ottaviano and David A. Ottaviano of Dubai, United Arab Emirates, was married Dec. 17 to Dustin Jacobs Brockner, a son of Audrey J. Brockner and Joel Brockner of New Rochelle, N.Y. Dr. Todd M. Poret, a cousin of the groom who became a minister of American Marriage Ministries for the occasion, officiated at Maritime Parc, an event space in Jersey City.
The couple met at Wesleyan University, from which they both graduated.
The bride, who will keep her name, is a second-year medical student at Rutgers. From 2009 to 2010, she was a Fulbright fellow at the University of Tetovo, in Tetovo, Macedonia, where she taught English reading and writing.
Her father is the principal and chief education officer of GEMS International School in Dubai. Her mother is a school counselor at GEMS Bradenton Prep Academy, also in Dubai.
The groom is a law clerk for Judge Lorna G. Schofield of the United States District Court for the Southern District of New York. He received a law degree from Yale.
His mother is a psychotherapist in New York and New Rochelle and an adjunct professor of clinical practice for social work at Hunter College. His father is the Phillip Hettleman Professor of Business at Columbia.
