Nicole Jessica Bochner and Adam William Malitz were married Dec. 3 at Temple Beth El in Cedarhurst, N.Y. Rabbi Gerald C. Skolnik performed the ceremony.
The bride, 29, is a project manager in New York for Horizon Media, where she manages media campaigns. She graduated from George Washington University.
She is a daughter of Debra C. Bochner and Jeffrey M. Bochner of Forest Hills, N.Y.
The groom, 29, is a commercial real estate broker in New York for Lee & Associates. He is the secretary and a board member of Manhattan Community Board 1 and is on the board of the Friends of Washington Market Park, a nonprofit neighborhood organization. He graduated from Hobart College.
He is the son of Rhona Drossman and Jeffrey Malitz of New York.
The couple met in 2014 through OkCupid.
