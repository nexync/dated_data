OUR NEW PRESIDENT (2018) on Amazon. Maxim Pozdorovkin’s documentary is made entirely with footage from Russian state-sponsored media, which he weaves together to tell the story of the 2016 presidential election. The film won a jury award for editing when it debuted at the Sundance Film Festival this year.
HOUR OF THE WOLF (1968) on Filmstruck. The news that Filmstruck, the streaming service partnership between Turner Classic Movies and the Criterion Collection library, is shutting down at the end of November is sad for film buffs everywhere. But there’s still time to revisit some highlights, including this surrealist horror film from Ingmar Bergman, which concerns a man (Max von Sydow) who, while vacationing on a small island with his wife (Liv Ullmann), begins to experience powerful delusions.
