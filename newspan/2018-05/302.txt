Kathryn Mary Lillian Rorer and Charles Isaac Doppelt were married May 19 at the Oceano Hotel and Spa in Half Moon Bay, Calif. The Rev. Don A. Mulford, a Presbyterian minister, officiated.
Mrs. Doppelt, 34, is a vice president for consultant relations, in San Mateo, Calif., at Fisher Investments, an asset manager.  She received a master’s degree in decision sciences from the London School of Economics and Political Science and an M.B.A. from Duke.
She is the daughter of Eleanor A. Rorer and John E. Rorer of New Rochelle, N.Y. The bride’s father retired as the chief financial officer of the American Museum of Natural History in New York. Her mother retired as the media relations manager at the United Hospital Fund, a health care, policy and programming organization in New York.
Mr. Doppelt, 38, is a managing director, overseeing sales, at Melting Point Solutions, a financial services advisory company in San Francisco. He graduated and received an M.B.A. from the University of California, Berkeley.
He is a son of Phillip H. Doppelt of San Jose, Calif., and the late Ilene J. Doppelt. The groom’s father retired as the owner of System Solutions Sales, a systems control software distributor that was in Santa Clara, Calif.; the groom’s mother was the office manager.
The couple, who met online through a Coffee Meets Bagel, had their first date in May 2015.
