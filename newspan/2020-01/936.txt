Corrections that appeared in print on Thursday, Jan. 16, 2020.
An article on Wednesday about Nissan’s financial troubles misstated the decline in Nissan sales in the United States last year. It was 10 percent, not 11 percent.
An obituary on Sunday about the civic leader and former congressman Frederick W. Richmond, who helped save Carnegie Hall from demolition, referred incorrectly to a developer’s plan for replacing the hall when it was torn down. The plan was to build an office building on the site, not an apartment building.
An obituary on Sunday about Sultan Qaboos bin Said of Oman at one point misstated his date of birth. As the obituary later stated correctly, it was Nov. 18, 1940, not Oct. 18.
