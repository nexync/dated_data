The artist Frédéric Pajak’s hybrid book “Uncertain Manifesto” is haunted by the philosopher’s encounters with fascism, among much else, as these illustrated panels show.
Frédéric Pajak is a writer and graphic artist, born in Suresnes, France, in 1955. The author of several books, he is also the editor of the illustrated biannual journal Les Cahiers Dessinés.
