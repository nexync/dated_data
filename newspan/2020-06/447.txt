Corrections that appeared in print on Saturday, June 27, 2020.
A map with an article on May 31 about a clash in the Himalayas between India and China included outdated boundaries for Jammu and Kashmir. The India-controlled area just west of the Line of Actual Control is the territory of Ladakh, not Jammu and Kashmir.
An article on Friday about “Jurassic Park” misidentified the actor who plays the businessman behind the theme park. He is Richard Attenborough, not David Attenborough.
