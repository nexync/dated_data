The human rights campaigner spoke to reporters in 1995, upon his return from China, where he had been detained on charges of espionage.
2. SOUNDBITE (English): Harry Wu, Chinese human rights campaigner “I am very happy to come back to my family, come back to my people. I just, in the last 2 or 3 hours realise, so many things happen over here. I saw everywhere has yellow ribbons. I am so proud I am an American citizen. Just like the Chinese government says, because you are an American citizen. It makes me very sad - if I am a common Chinese, I don’t think I would come back.
