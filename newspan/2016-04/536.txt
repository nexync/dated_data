The American Sloane Stephens outlasted Elena Vesnina to capture her third Women’s Tennis Association title this year with a 7-6 (4), 6-2 victory Sunday at the Volvo Car Open in Charleston, S.C.
The seventh-seeded Stephens squandered a 5-2 lead in the opening set before prevailing in a tiebreaker. She let three match points disappear in the contest’s final game before Vesnina hit her service return wide to secure the match.
■ Nine months after major wrist surgery, Argentina’s Juan Mónaco took advantage of the defending champion Jack Sock’s aching shoulder and leg cramps to rally for a 3-6, 6-3, 7-5 victory at the U.S. Men’s Clay Court Championship in Houston.
