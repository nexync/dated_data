The sky, the trees, the skin, the sleet, the sleep.
Show day how to blaze, let death drop its name.
Rita Dove is a Pulitzer Prize winner and a former poet laureate of the United States. She edited “The Penguin Anthology of Twentieth-Century American Poetry,” and her “Collected Poems: 1974-2004” was published in 2016. Dean Rader’s most recent collection of poetry, “Self Portrait as Wikipedia Entry,” was published in 2017 by Copper Canyon Press.
