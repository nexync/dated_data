In this episode of Modern Love: The Podcast, the Emmy Award-winning actor Tony Hale (“Veep,” “Arrested Development”) reads the essay “Just Friends? Let Me Read Between the Lines,” about the long process of letting an ex go.
You can also hear the episode on iTunes and Google Play Music.
The essay, published in October 2004, was the first-ever Modern Love column. It was written by Steve Friedman, a best-selling author based in New York.
Afterward, hear a conversation between Dan Jones and Cathi Hanauer, the editors of Modern Love when it began in 2004. Then listen as they catch up with Mr. Friedman.
