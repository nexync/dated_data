“Summer in the Forest” is an extraordinarily tender documentary that asks what it means to be human. Here, even the most gentle scenes raise mighty questions.
In the 1960s, Jean Vanier founded L’Arche, a community near Paris that has become home to many men and women with developmental disabilities. The filmmakers follow Mr. Vanier, now in his 80s, as he lives and works with those who have Down syndrome and other conditions.
