Corinne Elizabeth Hagans and Derrick Arthur Morrison were married March 24 at the Yards Brewery in Philadelphia. Nathan Frew, an Assemblies of God pastor and a friend of the couple, officiated.
Mrs. Morrison, 29, work at BounceU, a children’s amusement center. She is general manager of two locations, one in Elmsford, N.Y., the other in Nanuet, N.Y. She received an associate degree from Johnson & Wales University in Denver. She is a daughter of Ernest T. Hagans of Cherry Hill, N.J., and the late Pamela R. Hagans.
Mr. Morrison, also 29, is a New York-based actor, known as Derrick Arthur. His movie and television credits include “Delivery Man,” “People Places Things,” and “The Humbling.” He graduated with a degree in acting from the New York Conservatory for Dramatic Arts. He is the son of Deanna Morrison and Arthur Morrison of Grand Island, Neb.
