Savor the final weekends of summer. The Boffo Fire Island Performance Festival, which showcases experimental dance, performance and music, took place Aug. 26 to 28 on the L.G.B.T.-friendly beaches of the Pines. The festival featured a pop-up shop, estate tour, beachside performances and a V.I.P. dinner with Casey Spooner. On Aug. 27, VH1 Save the Music held a benefit in Sagaponack, N.Y., featuring a performance by Fifth Harmony. The event raised $1 million to promote instrumental music education in public schools.
