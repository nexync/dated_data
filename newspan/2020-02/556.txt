Corrections that appeared in print on Sunday, Feb. 23, 2020.
This weekend’s cover article about Ben Affleck misstates the year in which he was accused of groping a talk-show host, an act for which he apologized. It was 2003, not 2013.
An article last Sunday about Jordan Casteel and her show at the New Museum misstated the profession of Jiréh Breon Holder, who posed for Ms. Casteel. He is a writer and playwright, not an actor.
An article last Sunday about unique lodging options misstated the location of the Anantara Golden Triangle Elephant Camp & Resort. It is in Chiang Rai, Thailand, not Bangkok.
