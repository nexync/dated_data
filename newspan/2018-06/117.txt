Jane Carol Granville and Landen Tanner Saffles were married June 2 at St. Matthew Catholic Church in Franklin, Tenn. The Rev. Bruce Morrill, a Roman Catholic priest, performed the ceremony.
The bride, 29, recently graduated from the University of Tennessee College of Social Work in Nashville.
She is a daughter of Julie R. Granville and Kevin J. Granville of Sussex, N.J. The bride’s father is a senior staff editor at The New York Times. Her mother is a senior library assistant with the Sussex County Library System.
The groom, 31, is a licensed clinical social worker for the Defense Department at Fort Campbell Army Base in Kentucky. He also graduated from the University of Tennessee College of Social Work, from which he also received a master’s degree in that subject.
He is the son of Tammy G. Saffles and Joseph Saffles of Sweetwater, Tenn. His father, a pharmacist, owns the Little Drugs Family Wellness Center in Sweetwater.
The couple met on OkCupid.
