Phyllis Marie Lally, a daughter of Valerie Maggos Lally and Dr. Patrick T. Lally of Greensburg, Pa., was married Aug. 6 to Leonard William Seevers II, the son of Angela Tang Seevers of New York and Gary L. Seevers of Westport, Conn. Canon Alan Godfrey Dennis, an Episcopal priest, officiated at Blue Hill at Stone Barns in Pocantico Hills, N.Y.
The bride, 28, is the director of Kim Heirston Art Advisory, a firm in New York that helps clients build art collections. She graduated magna cum laude from Duke.
Her father is an ophthalmologist in Latrobe, Pa.
The groom, 33, is a managing director, overseeing principal investments, at Goldman Sachs in New York. He graduated cum laude from Middlebury College.
His father, who served on the Council of Economic Advisers to the White House during the Nixon, Ford and Carter administrations, retired as a partner in Goldman Sachs in New York.
The couple were introduced in January 2013 by a mutual friend in New York.
