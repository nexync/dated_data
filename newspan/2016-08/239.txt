Melissa Dina DeRosa and Matthew Lawrence Wing were married Aug. 27 at the Sagamore Resort in Bolton Landing, N.Y. Jed S. Rakoff, a United States District Judge for the Southern District of New York, officiated.
Ms. DeRosa, 33, is keeping her name. She is the chief of staff to Gov. Andrew M. Cuomo. Until March 2013, she was the deputy chief of staff to the state attorney general, Eric T. Schneiderman. She graduated from Cornell, where she also received a master's degree in public administration.
She is a daughter of Melody A. DeRosa of Chesapeake, Va., and Giorgio DeRosa of Slingerlands, N.Y. The bride’s father is a senior partner in Bolton-St. Johns, a Manhattan-based public affairs firm. Her mother, who worked in Fort Collins, Colo., retired as the West Coast regional director and education technology consultant for Texas Instruments.
Mr. Wing, also 33, works for Uber in Manhattan, where he oversees public relations for the northeastern United States. From October 2011 to June 2013, he was the deputy communications director for New York City, and from June 2013 to September 2014, he was press secretary for Governor Cuomo, and later became the communications director for the governor’s re-election campaign. He is on both Brooklyn Bridge Park Corporation boards. He graduated from Bard College in Annandale-on-Hudson, N.Y.
He is the son of Audrey Strauss and John R. Wing of Brooklyn Heights. The groom’s mother is a vice president and the chief legal officer for Alcoa, the aluminum company, in Manhattan. His father is a partner in the New York law firm Lankler Siffert & Wohl.
The couple met in 2013, when Ms. DeRosa was Governor Cuomo’s communications director, and Mr. Wing was his press secretary.
