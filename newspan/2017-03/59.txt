Lindsay Royce Alper and Andrew Russell Maller were married March 25 at the Plaza hotel in New York. Rabbi Michael White officiated.
Mrs. Maller, 28, is a senior financial analyst at Geller & Company, a corporate financial and advisory services firm in New York. She graduated from George Washington University. She is the daughter of Jodi L. Rosenthal of Muttontown, N.Y., and Stuart L. Alper of Purchase, N.Y.
The bride’s father is a managing director in the taxable fixed income department at Oppenheimer & Company in New York.
The bride is the stepdaughter of Wendy Alper and Robert D. Rosenthal. The bride’s stepfather is the chairman of First Long Island Investors, a private wealth management company in Jericho, N.Y. He was a chairman of the New York Islanders hockey team from 1992 to 1997. The bride’s stepmother is a real estate agent for Julia B. Fee Sotheby’s International Realty in Rye, N.Y.
Mr. Maller, 30, is an investment banker in New York in the mergers and acquisitions group at the Royal Bank of Canada. He also graduated from George Washington. He is a son of Lori F. Maller and Glenn J. Maller of Roslyn, N.Y. The groom’s mother is a teacher’s assistant for children with special needs at the East Hills School in Roslyn. His father is a principal of CGM Partners, a distributor of industrial supplies in New Rochelle, N.Y.
The couple met in June 2012 on a blind date in New York arranged by the bride’s stepsister.
