Lindsey Rebecca Mitrani and Charles Louis Harris are to be married March 26 at the University Club of New York. Rabbi Elliot Cosgrove is to officiate with Rabbi Sholom Lipskar, who is to perform the marriage blessings.
Ms. Mitrani, 25, is a third-year medical student at the Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons in New York. She graduated cum laude from Columbia.
She is a daughter of Dr. Janet C. Mitrani and Dr. Raul D. Mitrani of Bay Harbor Islands, Fla. The bride’s father is a director of clinical cardiac electrophysiology and an associate professor of medicine at the University of Miami school of medicine. Her mother is a dermatologist in Aventura, Fla.
Mr. Harris, 30, is an architect at Skidmore, Owings & Merrill, an architecture firm in New York. He graduated magna cum laude from the University of Pennsylvania and received a master’s degree in architecture from Harvard.
He is the son of Laurie L. Harris and Maury N. Harris of New York. The groom’s father retired as a managing director and chief economist at UBS securities, the Swiss bank, in New York.
The couple met in January 2014 through the dating app Hinge.
