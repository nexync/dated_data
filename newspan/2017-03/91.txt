Dr. Julia Blair Powell and James Erskine Love IV were married March 11 at the Figure Eight Island Yacht Club in Wilmington, N.C. The Rev. Dr. Scott Black Johnston, senior pastor of Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church in New York, officiated.
The bride, 27, is taking her husband’s name. She is a general dentistry resident at the Philadelphia V.A. Medical Center. She graduated with distinction and as a Morehead-Cain Scholar from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, from which she also received a doctor of dental surgery degree.
She is a daughter of Nan Stutzman Powell and William Douglas Powell Jr. of Wilmington. The bride’s father is the chief financial officer of D & L Shipping, a freight shipping company in Wilmington. Her mother is a librarian at Blair Elementary School, also in Wilmington.
The groom, 28, is studying for an M.B.A. at the University of Pennsylvania. He was until 2015 an associate at Kohlberg Kravis Roberts, a New York investment firm. He graduated from Duke.
He is a son of Sarah Ellen McIntire Love and James E. Love III of Atlanta. The groom’s father is chief executive of Printpack, a family-owned packaging company there. His mother retired as a lawyer with AT&T, also in Atlanta.
The couple met in November 2010, at a birthday party in Chapel Hill.
