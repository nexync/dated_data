Are Britain and the E.U. Ready for Brexit?
And so the divorce begins.
Britain’s Prime Minister Theresa May, invoking Article 50 of the Lisbon Treaty, on Wednesday formally announced her country’s plan to withdraw from the European Union.
Are Britain and the European Union ready to live apart?
The Telegraph suspects Europe is elated.
And it commends Mrs. May for astutely reading the winds.
Of course there are those, like Allister Heath of The Telegraph and the economist Patrick Minford — profiled in The Spectator in October — who think Brexit is a great thing for Britain. Mr. Minford thought Mrs. May should have pushed to leave the European Union earlier.
