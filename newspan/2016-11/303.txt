The Guggenheim International Gala, sponsored by Dior, was held on Nov. 17 and drew fashion luminaries and Hollywood stars. Gabrielle’s Angel Foundation for Cancer Research raised nearly $3.7 million at its annual ball on Nov. 21. More than 700 guests attended the American Museum of Natural History’s gala on Nov. 17.
