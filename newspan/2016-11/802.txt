Coffee and chocolate are a delicious pairing, as evidenced by this small box of truffles made by Delysia Chocolatier in Austin, Tex., using coffee from King’s Row in Hanover, N.H. King’s Row coffees come in blends devised by Craig Shelton, a James Beard Award winner who was the chef at the Ryland Inn in Whitehouse Station, N.J. The truffles have three flavors: dark chocolate with rum and orange; dark chocolate spiced with African seasonings; and milk chocolate, creamy and sweet, like Vietnamese iced coffee: Coffee Truffles by Delysia and King’s Row Coffee, $24 for a box of nine, kingsrowcoffee.com/confections.
