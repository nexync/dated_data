The Oscar-winning actress shows a new side in this scene, narrated by the director Tate Taylor.
The director Tate Taylor narrates a sequence from his film starring Octavia Spencer.
A group of teenagers picks the wrong adult to buy them alcohol in the horror thriller “Ma.” This early scene features Octavia Spencer as Sue Ann, who encounters Maggie (Diana Silvers) outside a liquor store with her friends in a van nearby.
Narrating the scene, the director Tate Taylor explains how he uses it to disarm the audience, giving a sense of innocence, but including clues that foreshadow the dark turns the movie will take.
