Guy Trebay’s description of jackets made from the skin of baby cows is out of touch. Rock stars aren’t fashion models. We don’t follow trends. To play in a rock band is already an act of subversion, and wearing leather and fur isn’t subversive; it’s for old ladies who live in the past.
The writer, a founding member of the rock band The Pretenders, is an honorary director of People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA).
