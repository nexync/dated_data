Your chances for romance may be directly affected by where you live. That’s what a recent posting by Trulia.com suggests, anyway.
Using census data from the 100 largest metropolitan areas in the country, the company has created a quiz to help readers identify the cities where the odds are best for finding a mate — taking into account preferences about gender, age, education level and hours of weekly employment. Those with a previous marriage can be eliminated as well. Only singles over the age of 18 are included in the calculation.
