What Are Critics Saying About the ‘Good Wife’ Spinoff, ‘The Good Fight’?
CBS will air the series premiere of “The Good Fight” on Sunday, Feb. 19, at 8 p.m., when it will also become available on the network’s paid streaming service, CBS All Access. A second episode will become available on All Access at 8:30 that same night, and new installments of the ten-episode season will appear there exclusively every Sunday. But is “The Good Fight” good enough to merit shelling out for yet another monthly streaming subscription? Here’s what critics have to say.
“Baranski brings a heartbreaking rawness to her performance as Diane, who never got enough meaningful screen time on ‘The Good Wife,’” writes Variety’s Maureen Ryan.
