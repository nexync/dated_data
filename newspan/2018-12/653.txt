The Book Review’s poetry editor, Gregory Cowles, discusses Tracy K. Smith’s essay about political poetry and more from this week’s special issue.
On this week’s podcast, Gregory Cowles, the Book Review’s poetry editor (as well as its senior editor), talks about the wide array of poets featured in the issue, as well as Smith’s centerpiece essay.
Also on this week’s episode, Maria Russo, the Book Review’s children’s books editor, talks about the year’s best books for younger readers; Alexandra Alter has news from the publishing world; and Gal Beckerman, Concepción de León and John Williams talk about what people are reading. Pamela Paul is the host.
