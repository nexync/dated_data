Duterte Thinks He Can Bomb Islamists Into Submission. He Cannot.
Above all, the Philippine military is corrupt. And United States assistance has only abetted that corruption by creating a moral hazard. With America handing the Philippines an average of nearly $40 million a year in military assistance between 2002 and 2012, local security forces have had a stake in not finishing off Abu Sayyaf or other radical groups.
