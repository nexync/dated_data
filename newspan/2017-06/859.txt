Megha Chandresh Patel, the daughter of Bhavana C. Patel and Chandresh J. Patel of Newtown, Pa., was married June 2 to Shamil Pratap Kotecha, the son of Daxa Kotecha and Pratap G. Kotecha of Royal Palm Beach, Fla. Pandit Prabhakar Sharma, a Hindu priest, officiated at Cipriani 42nd Street in New York.
Mrs. Kotecha, 28, is an associate in New York at the litigation firm Wagner Berkow. She graduated from N.Y.U. and received a law degree from the New England School of Law.
Mr. Kotecha, 30, is an associate in the corporate department at the New York law firm Cravath, Swaine & Moore. He graduated with high distinction from the University of Michigan and received a law degree cum laude from Harvard.
The couple met in January 2013 at a party in Cambridge, Mass.
