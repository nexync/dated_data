Caroline Gordon Cannon and John Arthur Martin were married June 17. Andrew W. Hoffmann, an uncle of the bride who became a Universal Life minister for the event, officiated at the Country Club of Virginia in Richmond.
Ms. Cannon, 29, is a freelance field producer for documentary films, and was until November an associate producer and a field producer for the Vice Media digital media and broadcasting company in Brooklyn. She graduated from the University of Virginia.
She is a daughter of Barbara L. Gordon and W. Stephen Cannon of Bethesda, Md. The bride’s father is the chairman of Constantine Cannon, a Manhattan law firm, and is the managing partner of its Washington office. Her mother is a trustee of the American Folk Art Museum in Manhattan.
Mr. Martin, also 29, is a portfolio manager at Highbridge Capital Management, a hedge fund in Manhattan that is a subsidiary of the asset management division of JPMorgan Chase & Company. He graduated summa cum laude from Duke.
He is a son of Anne Hancock Martin and Robert C. Martin of Fort Lauderdale, Fla. The groom’s mother retired this past week as a speech language pathologist at East Campus Elementary of the Pembroke Pines Charter School, in Pembroke Pines, Fla. His father is a founder of Martin Bennis, a Fort Lauderdale law firm.
The couple met in 2012 through friends at a rooftop party in Manhattan.
