Anna Elisabeth Gabbay and Yosef Tzvi Dovid Adelstein are to be married June 25 in Brooklyn. Rabbi Salman Levitin is to officiate at the Brooklyn Botanic Garden.
Mrs. Adelstein, 28, is an associate at Marshall Conway & Bradley, a law firm in Manhattan. She graduated from the University of Miami and received her law degree from Fordham.
She is a daughter of Katherine A. Gabbay of Westport, Conn., and Edmond Gabbay of Brooklyn. The bride’s father is a partner at Arnold & Porter Kaye Scholer in Manhattan. Her mother is an English teacher at Ridgefield High School in Ridgefield, Conn. The bride is also the stepdaughter of Kenneth W. Franz and Robbin B. Slade.
Mr. Adelstein, 34, who goes by Yussel, is the chief operating officer in Woodhaven, Queens, of Finest Foods Distributing, a company that imports and distributes Caribbean and Hispanic food products. Previously, he worked as a private chef. He graduated from the Jerusalem Culinary Institute.
He is a son of Caral M. Adelstein and Steven Adelstein of Crown Heights, Brooklyn. The groom’s mother is a homeopathic private practitioner in Crown Heights. His father is an owner at Finest Foods Distributing.
The couple met in 2015 on Fire Island over Memorial Day weekend.
