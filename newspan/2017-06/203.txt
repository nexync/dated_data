Gwen Perlman and Warren Randolph Winter were married June 17 at Battery Gardens in New York. Rabbi David Greenberg of Temple Shaaray Tefila of Bedford Corners, N.Y., officiated.
The bride, 53, is the university capital budget director at the City University of New York. She graduated from N.Y.U., from which she also received a master’s degree in political science and an M.B.A.
She is the daughter of Sue L. Perlman of East Hampton, N.Y., and the late Harold Perlman.
The groom, 60, is a manager of information technology at CUNY’s Advanced Science Research Center. He graduated from Binghamton University and received a master’s degree in management from N.Y.U. He was a widower.
He is a son of Pearl K. Winter of Nyack, N.Y., and the late Charles Winter.
The couple met in 2013 through JDate.
