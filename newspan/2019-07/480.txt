
Rita Dove is a Pulitzer Prize winner and a former poet laureate of the United States. She edited “The Penguin Anthology of Twentieth-Century American Poetry,” and her “Collected Poems: 1974-2004” was published in 2016. Kim Stafford is a writer and teacher in Portland, Ore. He is the author of a dozen books of poetry and prose, most recently “Wild Honey, Tough Salt,” published by Red Hen Press in April.
