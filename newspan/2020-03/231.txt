Sarah Ashley Schiear and Jared Brett Spiegel were married March 7 at Race & Religious, a rental property in New Orleans. Rabbi Erdheim officiated.
Mrs. Spiegel, 35, is the founder of Salt House, a lifestyle brand and online shop based in New York. She graduated from the University of Miami.
She is the daughter of Lana D. Belcher and the late Charles F. Schiear III, who lived in Nokomis, Fla.
Mr. Spiegel, 33, is a co-founder and the chief operating officer of Highfield, a creative agency in New York. He graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and received a law degree from Brooklyn Law School.
He is the son of Bruce A. Spiegel and Barbara M. Spiegel, of New York.
The couple met in 2016 in New York when Mrs. Spiegel was looking for a lawyer while she was starting her company, and a networking contact recommended Mr. Spiegel.
