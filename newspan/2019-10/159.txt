Henchmen forced a patrol of armed forces to release Ovidio Guzmán López after he had been captured.
The latest family member to escape apprehension — El Chapo’s son, Ovidio Guzmán López — managed his own feat of government humiliation this week, when cartel henchmen forced a patrol of at least 30 members of government forces to release him after he had been captured.
Elisabeth Malkin and Paulina Villegas contributed reporting.
